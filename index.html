Who we are

Eight students. One school.
A crate full of old records.

We're a team of just under ten Year 10 students at Radnor House Sevenoaks, running REplay as our company for the Young Enterprise programme. Our name starts with RE to mark Radnor Enterprise, and it also emphasizes recycling and replaying old records to keep them out of landfill.

We split ourselves across three departments - Finance, Production and Marketing - and run REplay the way a real small business has to: tight budgets, real suppliers, real customers.

We took our first batch of clocks to Spitalfields Market in London, set up a stall among working market traders, and sold directly to the public. It's where we learned what people actually want to hang on their wall.

Our impact

Every clock is one less record in landfill.

Vinyl is PVC - it doesn't biodegrade, and most warped or scratched records end up thrown away rather than played. We intercept them before that happens, and turn something unplayable into something useful for decades.
